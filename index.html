About Me

Designer & Problem Solver

I don't just make things look good — I make them work better for the people using them.

Most apps fail not because of bad code — but because someone skipped the user. That's the problem I exist to solve.

I'm Venkatesh — a UI/UX Designer with 3+ years of end-to-end product design experience. I work across the full process: user research, information architecture, wireframes, mobile-first interfaces, design systems, and developer-ready Figma handoffs.

At BeamX TechLabs, I've designed across an industrial IoT app, an AI chatbot with source citations, and an e-commerce platform — all from scratch. I work directly with clients to translate unclear briefs into structured design problems and run cross-functional workshops to keep dev and design aligned.

I don't treat handoff as the finish line. I stay involved through implementation to make sure what ships matches what was designed.

How I Work

My Design Process

Every project follows a structured approach — but stays flexible enough to adapt to what the problem actually needs.

🔍

Discover & Research

User interviews, client briefs, competitive analysis. I understand the real problem before touching a frame.

🗺️

Define & Architect

Personas, user flows, sitemaps. Structure the information before designing the interface.

✏️

Design & Prototype

Wireframes to high-fidelity mockups in Figma. Design systems and interactive prototypes for every screen.

🧪

Test & Iterate

Usability testing with 5–8 users per round. Feedback loops that improve the product before it ships.

🚀

Handoff & Implement

Developer-ready specs, component documentation, and staying involved through implementation. Handoff isn't the finish line.
