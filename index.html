About Me

Designer & Problem Solver

I don't just make things look good, I make them work better for the people using them.

Most interfaces fail not because of bad code, but because someone skipped the user. That's the gap I fill.

I'm Venkatesh, a Product Designer with 3+ years owning design end-to-end across enterprise SaaS, AI products, and mobile applications. I've shipped 700+ production screens across 4 live products, AI talent platforms, logistics systems, marketplace solutions, and industrial IoT apps.

At BeamX TechLabs, I work directly with Product Managers, Developers, and business stakeholders, translating complex requirements into structured design decisions. I've reduced revision cycles from 4 rounds to 2 and built a 40+ component Figma system used across all products.

I stay involved through implementation. Handoff isn't the finish line.

How I Work

My Design Process

Structured enough to be reliable. Flexible enough to fit what each problem actually needs.

🔍

Discover & Research

User interviews, client briefs, competitive analysis. I understand the real problem before touching a frame.

🗺️

Define & Architect

Personas, user flows, sitemaps, information architecture. Structure before surface.

✏️

Design & Prototype

Wireframes through high-fidelity Figma. Interactive prototypes that can be tested, not just presented.

🧪

Test & Iterate

Usability sessions with 5–8 users per round. Feedback loops before shipping.

🚀

Handoff & Implement

Developer-ready specs and staying involved during build. Handoff isn't where my job ends.

Graphic Work

Brand assets, campaign creatives, and event materials consistent visual identity across every touchpoint.
